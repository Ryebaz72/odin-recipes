Lasagna

Description

A really delicious vegetable lasagna with a creamy white sauce.

Serves 8 and is ready to eat in just over an hour.

Ingredients

  • 9 Lasagna strips
  • 60 grams Margarine
  • 35 grams Plain Flour
  • Pinch Salt
  • Pinch Pepper
  • 225 ml Milk
  • 225 ml Vegetable Soup
  • 2 tablespoons white wine
  • 50 grams grated Parmesan cheese
  • 120 grams light ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 egg white
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 300 grams coarsely shredded carrots
  • 300 coarsely chopped courgette
  • 250 grams frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
  • 115 grams shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Drain.
  2. Preheat oven to 190 degrees C (375 degrees F).
  3. Melt margarine in a pan over low heat; cook and stir flour, salt, and pepper into the melted margarine until smooth and bubbling, 2 to 3 minutes. Add milk and broth into flour mixture, stirring constantly; bring sauce to a boil. Stir wine into sauce and remove from heat. Stir Parmesan cheese into sauce until smooth.
  4. Whisk ricotta cheese, egg, and egg white together in a bowl.
  5. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat; saute carrots, courgettes and spinach until just becoming tender, 5 to 10 minutes. Stir vegetable mixture into ricotta mixture.
  6. Layer 1/3 the lasagna strips, 1/3 the ricotta-vegetable mixture, and 1/3 the Parmesan sauce into a large baking dish; repeat layering 2 more times with remaining ingredients, ending with a layer of mozzarella cheese.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is lightly browned and bubbling, about 30 minutes.
